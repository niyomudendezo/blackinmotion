SERVICES

Back In Motion Physio We provide a range of services aimed at promoting, maintaining, and restoring optimal physical function through home visit habilitation.

1.Assessment and Evaluation
We assess to identify the patient's condition, including medical history, physical examination, and functional testing.

2.Therapeutic Exercise
We provide and guide patients through specific exercises to improve strength, flexibility, endurance, and overall physical function of patients.

3.Manual Therapy
We do Hands-on techniques such as joint mobilization, manipulation, and soft tissue release to improve joint flexibility and muscle streangth.

4.Massage Therapy
Soft tissue manipulation and massage techniques to improve circulation, reduce muscle tension, and promote relaxation.
