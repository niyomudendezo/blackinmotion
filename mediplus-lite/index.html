What We do

Home visit physiotherapy provides personalized care, addressing patients' physical needs in the comfort of their homes. It promotes convenience, improves compliance, and enhances rehabilitation outcomes through tailored interventions.

Back and Neck Pain

Acute pain that comes on suddenly or chronic back and neck pain that lasts for months or years restrict normal functioning. Back In Motion Physio eases pain and improves mobility and fuction.

Sports Injuries

Specific types of sports injuries, like sprains ,strains, overuse injury,stress fracture and post-exessive exercises Muscular pain,can be treated through a suitable rehabilitation plan to promote safe recovery.

Arthritis

Arthritis is a digenerative disorders that affect the joints, causing inflammation, pain, stiffness, and swelling.
Back In Motion Physio plays a crucial role in the management and treatment of arthritis, aiming to reduce pain, improve joint function, and enhance overall quality of life for individuals with this condition.

Post Surgery rehabilitation

Post-surgery physiotherapy, also known as post-operative rehabilitation we focuse on helping individuals recover and regain optimal function after undergoing surgical procedures.

Neurological disorders

Too much stress due to the overuse of some parts of your body causes swelling or you feel tenderness in those areas. Rage of motion gets impeded, which can be revived with therapy. Likewise, physical therapy will achieve better extension or flexion.
