Mission

My mission at Back in motion Physiotherapy is to provide professional and caring physiotherapy services to help my patients lead happy, vibrant and productive lives

vision

My vision is to be a to be a leader in providing physiotherapy care that is evidence-based and imbued with empathy

My Core Values

These are the values that I strive for at my clinic:

Professional

I provide professional service in a courteous manner. I strive the respect my patients time commitments and keep a clean and neat clinic environment that is conducive to health and healing.

Caring

I treat my patients with care and dignity. I maintain a kind and warm atmosphere in the clinic. I take the time to listen to them and answer their questions. I provide 100% of the physiotherapy treatment myself for the patients I see at my clinic.

Patient Feedback

I am committed to keeping an open dialogue with my patients, as well as ensuring they receive the highest quality care at my clinic. Please feel free to contact me and leave me a comment on any aspect of your care. I sincerely appreciate your feedback!
